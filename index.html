About CTM Tech

Eighteen years of enterprise habits

CTM Tech has served Hertfordshire since 2016. Behind it sits 18 years inside corporate IT environments — the kind where downtime is counted in pounds per minute and a badly segmented network becomes a board-level problem.

That background shapes everything here. Most small businesses inherit their IT by accident: a router from the broadband provider, a firewall nobody has logged into since install, laptops running whatever came in the box. It works right up until it very much doesn't. We bring corporate standards down to SMB scale and SMB budgets — and we document what we build, so you're never held hostage by the only person who understands it.

The same principles carry into the home. A smart home is an estate with fewer users and higher expectations, and it deserves a properly designed network underneath it just as much as an office does.
